Our Story

Our ever rising consumption of goods and growing throwaway culture has taken a toll on the Earth’s resources. With the aim of minimizing the damage of our consumption patterns and with this in mind, innovative manufacturing processes were developed which would enable used newspapers to be transformed into furniture.

Papertiles concept was conceived from months of research, cross-functional collaborations and experimentation resulted in the creation of the Papertiles material, opening endless possibilities to make a positive environmental impact globally.

Our products is 100% natural – Handcrafted from a mixture of 100% newspaper and our secret glue – a bioplastic mixture that was developed in house. Colour is applied using an all natural milk-based paint that is free of lead or harsh chemicals to mitigate any harmful VOCs. A waterproofing coat is also provided to prolong the lifespan of our products.
